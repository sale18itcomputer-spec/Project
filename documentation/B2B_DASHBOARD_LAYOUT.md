## 🔄 Switching Between Modes

When you toggle between B2C and B2B:

**Switching to B2B:**
- Metric cards reduce from 6 to 2
- Charts reduce from 5 to 1
- Layout becomes more compact
- Focus on core B2B metrics

**Switching to B2C:**
- All metric cards appear (6 total)
- All charts appear (5 total)
- Full dashboard experience
- Complete analytics view

## 💡 Why This Design?

### B2B Mode Philosophy
- **Focused**: Only essential metrics
- **Clean**: Less visual clutter
- **Fast**: Quicker load times
- **Relevant**: B2B-specific data only

### What B2B Users Need
1. **Pipeline Count** - How many opportunities
2. **Company Count** - How many B2B clients
3. **Pipeline Status** - Distribution of opportunities
4. **Filtering** - Find specific pipelines/companies

### What B2B Users Don't Need
- Contact logs (not tracked in B2B)
- Site surveys (B2C-specific)
- Meetings (B2C-specific)
- Revenue charts (until B2B sale orders exist)
- Customer rankings (simplified in B2B)
